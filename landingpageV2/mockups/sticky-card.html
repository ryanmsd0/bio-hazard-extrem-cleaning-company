When to call us

Blood, bodily fluids and other biohazardous materials carry serious health risks — bloodborne pathogens can survive on surfaces for days. If an accident, injury or traumatic event has left any biological material in your home or business, don't clean it yourself.

Our crews are trained to OSHA bloodborne-pathogen standards, arrive in unmarked vehicles if you prefer, and treat you and your property with total respect.

Situations we handle

  • Accidents and injuries involving blood or bodily fluids
  • Crime scenes, after law enforcement has released the property
  • Unattended deaths and decomposition cleanup
  • Sewage backups and contaminated water
  • Rodent droppings and infestation biohazards

Insurance & cost

Most homeowner and commercial policies cover biohazard cleanup. We bill your insurance directly — in most cases you pay nothing out of pocket. If your situation isn't covered, you'll get a free written estimate before any work begins.

You can verify any California contractor at cslb.ca.gov. Hiring an unlicensed cleaner can put your insurance claim at risk.
