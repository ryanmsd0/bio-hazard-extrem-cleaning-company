7

Movement & animation

How much movement do you want? Given the sensitive topic, we keep it tasteful — never flashy. (All motion can be turned off for users who prefer no animation.)

Level A · Minimal

Barely any

Static, fast, no-nonsense. Only essential states (hover, menu).
  • Buttons react on hover
  • FAQ open/close
Level B · Smooth

Gentle & modern

Soft fade-ins as you scroll. Feels premium, stays calm.
  • Everything in Minimal, plus:
  • Sections fade up on scroll
  • Numbers count up (24/7, <60 min)
  • Buttons lift on hover
  • Before/after photo slider
Level C · Cinematic

More dramatic

Bigger scroll effects. Eye-catching but heavier.
  • Everything in Smooth, plus:
  • Photo parallax & zoom
  • Pinned/scroll-told sections
  • Animated process timeline
