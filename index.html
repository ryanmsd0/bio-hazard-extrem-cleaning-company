FAQ

What people ask us most

How much does biohazard cleanup cost?
In most cases, homeowner and commercial insurance policies usually cover biohazard and trauma cleanup, and we bill your insurer directly. If your situation isn’t covered, you get a free estimate — on-site or remote, within our service area — before any work begins, and we accept cash or card. No surprises.
Do you bill insurance directly?
Yes. We bill all major homeowner and commercial insurers directly (Assignment of Benefits) and handle the paperwork for you.
How fast can you arrive?
Our crews are on call 24/7 and typically respond within 90 minutes across Los Angeles County.
Is the service discreet?
Absolutely. Unmarked vehicles are available, everything is kept fully confidential, and our crews are trained to work with compassion and dignity.
Are your technicians certified and licensed?
Yes. We are licensed, bonded and IICRC-certified, and we follow EPA standards on every job. Hiring an unlicensed cleaner can put your insurance claim at risk.
What areas do you serve?
We serve Los Angeles and surrounding areas including Burbank, Glendale, Pasadena, Inglewood, Hollywood, Santa Monica, Long Beach and more.
