What it is

What is fire damage restoration, and when do you need it?

After a fire, the damage rarely stops at what burned. Soot, smoke residue, lingering odors and water from firefighting can keep harming your property and your health for days. Fire damage restoration is the complete process of cleaning, deodorizing and rebuilding your space to a safe, livable condition.

The sooner we start, the more we can save. Our certified crews respond fast and handle everything, so you can focus on your family.

Fire damage restoration: your questions answered

Is fire damage covered by insurance?
In most cases yes. Fire damage is covered by most homeowner and commercial policies. We work directly with your insurance and can bill them for you.
How quickly can you respond?
Our crews are on call 24/7 and typically arrive on-site in under 60 minutes across Los Angeles County. The faster we start, the more we can save.
Can smoke odor really be removed?
Yes. We use professional deodorization and air treatment to remove smoke odor at the source, not just mask it.
Do you handle the water damage too?
Yes. We extract the water used to put out the fire and fully dry the structure to prevent mold, as part of the same service.
Are your technicians certified?
Yes. We are licensed, insured and IICRC-certified, and we follow OSHA and EPA standards on every job.
